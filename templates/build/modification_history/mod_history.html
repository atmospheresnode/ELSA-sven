Modification History

In order to keep track of changes that happen as labels are updated, PDS4 allows providers to insert Modification History with Modification Details so users can track the history of any major edits. ELSA inserts an initial one to mark the time of creation of the original label templates (Create Modification History). You are welcome to add more in the following Bundle View, if you need to update the labels for later iterations.
