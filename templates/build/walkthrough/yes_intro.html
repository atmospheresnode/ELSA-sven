Welcome to the Guided Walkthrough

Follow the step-by-step process below to build your bundle. You may skip any step during the walkthrough and still add or edit information later from the main bundle interface.

{% if bundle.bundle_type != 'External' %}
Alias

PDS4 allows providers to add alternative names or identifiers as 'Aliases' for the bundle. These are optional and can be added later. If you know you have alternate names, add them now or skip this step.

Modification History

Track changes in your bundle by adding Modification History details. An initial record marks creation, but you can add more updates as needed.

{% endif %}
Citation Information

Add authors and editors to credit label creators. Authors should be considered the data provider (and collaborators) — there will be a place for their Institution(s) and ORCID(s). Editors should be your PDS-ATM contact(s). Organizations are group entities that act as an author (e.g., Science Teams) — set Organizations to 0 unless you plan to include a large group.

Context Products

Categorize investigations such as missions or campaigns. Selecting one filters components like instruments and targets relevant to that investigation.
