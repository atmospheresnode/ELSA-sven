Citation Information

Anyone creating labels should be able to get credit for their work. Citation Information portion of the labels allows authors and editors to be added to a project. For this version of PDS4 authors and editors can be listed following the pattern, Last Name, First Initial, with multiples separated by a semi-colon. (e.g., Sanchez, R.; Smith, M.)
